Cheating Jack

This page showcases the exciting features of Cheating Jack, a Black Jack inspired game with a twist of cheating made in Unity. This was made for the submission of Mini Jam 150: Magic

Demonstration Video


Features

Made in Under 24 Hours

A fun and relaxing card game inspired by Black Jack with a twist created in just under 24 hours, showcasing rapid development and creativity.

Card Deck System

A simple yet effective card deck system with randomized cards from 1 until 11.

Simple Cheating System

Players can attempt to cheat, adding a fun twist to the gameplay. Shown from the cheating bar, if the player cheats too much, the player loses. If the player wins without cheating, the cheating bar decreases.
