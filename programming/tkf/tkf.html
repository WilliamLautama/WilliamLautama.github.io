The King's Follower

This page showcases the exciting features of The King's Follower, a click-based roguelike with simplicity in its mechanics but depth in its polish made in 1 week. This was made for the submission of Unity Jam #2.

Demonstration Video


Features

Click-Based Combat

Simplicity in its combat system where you click the enemy to damage them.

Vaeriety of Enemies

3 variety of enemies which are the slime, ranged enemies, and golem enemies.

Upgrade System

Choose from 3 different upgrades where you can either heal / increase the king's max health points or add damage to the attack.
