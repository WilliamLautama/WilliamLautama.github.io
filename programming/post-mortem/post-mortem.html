Post Mortem

Explore the world of Post Mortem, a game featuring two unique endings and basic 3D object interactions where you are dead and you are trying to figure out who killed you. This was made as part of my Immersive Environments assignment in Unity with Playmaker.

Demonstration Video


Features

Two Ending Mechanic

Experience multiple outcomes based on your choices, leading to two distinct game endings.

Basic 3D Object Interactions

Engage with interactive 3D objects to solve puzzles and progress through the game.
