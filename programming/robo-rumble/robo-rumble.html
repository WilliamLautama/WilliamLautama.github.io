Robo Rumble

Robo Rumble is a creative 3D platforming local multiplayer game that makes player 1 and 2 fight against each other while also trying to survive.

Demonstration Video


Features

Local Multiplayer System

A system that allows players to play against each other locally with controllers.

Dynamic Grid-Based Level Builder

Levels are made on the fly dynamically based on what the players choose.

Simple Movement System

Simple movement system that involves jumping in a third person manner to avoid traps while also flying to put traps in the grid.
