Blockout Shooter

A third-person shooter prototype featuring movement-focused mechanics and technical features made in Unreal Engine 5.2. This was made as part my assignment for Games Programming 2 in Monash University.

Demonstration Video


Features

Multiplayer Networking Implementation

Multiplayer networking is implemented for seamless communication between players over the network.

Third Person Wall Run Mechanic

The wall run mechanic has been adapted for third-person perspective, offering a dynamic gameplay experience.

Poison Trail

A poison trail mechanic allows the player to leave a damaging trail behind, harming enemies that come into contact.

Balloon Trap

The balloon trap mechanic lets the player attach balloons to enemies, causing them to float and become vulnerable to attacks.

Chaos Mace

The Chaos Mace is a weapon that creates a massive chaotic area around the player when used, pulling in enemies and objects.

Hit Particle Effect Implementation

A hit particle effect was implemented to visualize impacts on enemies or objects when hit by the playerâ€™s weapon.

Collision Enabled Particle Effect

Particle effects trigger on collision with objects or enemies, enhancing the visual impact of attacks and gameplay.
