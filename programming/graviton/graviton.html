Graviton

A gravity based puzzle platformer game where you have to finish the level as fast as you can while switching gravity. This was made for a submission of Speedjam #3 and was made in Unreal Engine 5.3 using Blueprints.

Demonstration Video


Features

Made in Under 3 Days

Graviton was developed in just under 3 days, demonstrating rapid game design and creativity.

5th Place at Game Jam (Speedjam #3)

Graviton achieved 5th place at the SpeedJam competition, showcasing its quality and innovation.

Online Leaderboard System

A fully online leaderboard system is integrated to keep count of the player's time based on the player's name.

Simple Movement Mechanics

Master the simple yet engaging movement mechanics and navigate through challenging levels.
